# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Ataç Biology (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (hereinafter referred to as "the User" or "Users") of the Ataç Biology mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the Application"). 

## 1. Information Collection

The Company may collect several types of information from and about Users of the Application, including:

*   **Account Information:** Upon the creation of an account, the Application collects the User's email address, name, and password for authentication purposes. This information is securely managed via Firebase Authentication.
*   **User-Generated Content:** Users may upload text, files, and images (such as profile pictures or educational attachments) while utilizing the Application. Such content is securely stored using Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage.
*   **Camera and Photo Library Access:** To facilitate image uploads, the Application requests permission to access the User's device camera and photo library. The Company only accesses, collects, and stores images that the User explicitly selects for upload to the Application.
*   **Device and Usage Data:** The Company may collect anonymous diagnostic and usage data to monitor the performance of the Application and address technical issues.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify the User. The Company shares necessary data with these services strictly to ensure the proper functionality of the Application. The privacy policies of these third-party service providers are applicable:

*   [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   [Firebase Authentication](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) (utilized for account security)
*   [Cloud Firestore & Firebase Storage](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) (utilized for data and content storage)
*   [Adapty](https://adapty.io/privacy) (utilized for the management and verification of in-app subscriptions and purchases)

## 3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The Application offers in-app purchases and subscriptions. All payment processing is securely executed by Google Play. The Company does not collect, process, or store the User's credit card or financial details. Third-party services, such as Adapty, are utilized strictly to verify subscription status and grant access to premium educational content.

## 4. Utilization of Information

The Company utilizes the collected data for the following purposes:
*   To provide, maintain, and enhance the functionality of the Application.
*   To manage User accounts, verify identity, and provide customer support.
*   To process and deliver premium content in accordance with User subscriptions.
*   To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or software bugs.
